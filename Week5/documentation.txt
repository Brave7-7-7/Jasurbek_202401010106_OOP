2.1 Why are the variables declared as private?
To protect the data from being accessed or modified directly encapsulation.

2.2 What happens if the variables are declared public?
Any class can access and modify the variables directly, making the program less secure and harder to maintain.

2.3 Why do we use getters and setters?
Getters retrieve the values of private variables, while setters update them safely and provide controlled access.
